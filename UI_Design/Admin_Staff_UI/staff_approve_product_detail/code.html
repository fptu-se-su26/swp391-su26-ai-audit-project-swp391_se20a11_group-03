Patek Philippe Ref. 5270G Perpetual Calendar Chronograph

Description & Condition Report

This Patek Philippe Ref. 5270G-001 is a masterpiece of modern horology, representing the evolution of the brand's legendary perpetual calendar chronograph lineage. Housed in a 41mm 18k white gold case, it features a stunning silvery-white dial with oxidized gold applied hour markers and tachymeter scale.

The condition of this timepiece is exemplary (Grade A+). It exhibits minimal to no signs of wear, having been stored in a climate-controlled vault since its original purchase in 2014. The movement, Caliber CH 29-535 PS Q, has been inspected by our in-house master watchmakers and is performing well within COSC specifications with a robust power reserve.

The lot is accompanied by its original Patek Philippe lacquered wood presentation box, Certificate of Origin, and all original documentation. The black hand-stitched alligator strap remains in unworn condition, secured by the original 18k white gold fold-over clasp.
