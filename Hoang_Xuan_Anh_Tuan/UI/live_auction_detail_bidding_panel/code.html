history_edu Item Description

Presented here is a breathtaking example of the legendary Rolex Cosmograph Daytona, Reference 6239, dating to approximately 1968. This specific piece is revered among collectors for its pristine "Paul Newman" exotic dial, characterized by the distinct art-deco style numerals in the sub-registers and the stepped outer track.

The pump pushers and steel bezel graduated to 200 units per hour remain sharp, indicating minimal polishing over its lifetime. The watch embodies the golden era of motorsport timing and stands as a paragon of vintage horology, offering an unrepeatable opportunity to acquire a cornerstone piece for any serious collection.
